Batman is a fictional character created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger in the DC Comics and movies as well as one of the most famous superheroes in DC. Batman is the secret identity of the very rich and successful businessman Bruce Wayne. Batman is one of the most famous and popular superheroes. More than any other character, Batman understands the pain and the struggle that everyone goes through. He may have amazing gadgets and an indomitable intellect, but his greatest strength is his empathy and care for others.
